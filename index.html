In keeping with my goal to dedicate this project to Sasha, I am going to tell you three things about her.

Breed

She is a lhasa apso. A non-sporting breed of small dogs originating from Tibet where they were highly regarded watchdogs in the palaces and monasteries of their mountainous homeland.

Behaviour

She is a very shy dog, so she tends to be hostile towards unfamiliar faces. This is in part due to her protective nature, but she warms up and is really playful once she gets comfortable.

Appetite

She eats a lot lol. Her appetite is high, and so she's bigger than the average size for a 9-month old lhasa, but she is not overweight and she is very healthy
